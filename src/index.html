About Me

My name is Lesley, and I am a fully qualified beauty and holistic therapist practicing in Poole, where I set up my home salon to recognise a life-long passion.

My journey to help people to feel good began when I enrolled in asports therapy college course where I studied massage, nutrition and exercise. I decided to combine my training with holistic treatments after experiencing their numerous benefits for myself.

To complement these therapies, I offer a range of beauty treatments which are designed to enhance the overall feeling of wellbeing. These treatments all utilise cruelty-free products in line with my ethos.

Before commencing any treatment, I carry out a consultation to discuss the treatment you would like, its suitability for you and the process involved. Treatments are carried out in a relaxing, calm environment where time, care and attentiveness are essential for the overall experience.

Facial treatments

Express facial - £30

Skin is double cleansed removing any build-up of oils and debris before a mask is applied to clear blocked pores. This is then followed by a moisturiser and SPF.

Deluxe facial - £40

This treatment will freshen the skin with a gentle cleanser followed by removal of dead skin cells. A relaxing mask will infuse the skin with nutrients before the application of hydrating moisturisers and a SPF.

The products which I use are suitable for any skin type and condition as they are chosen according to the clientʼs skin care needs.

High frequency facial - £50

This treatment combines the deluxe facial with a high frequency treatment. During the treatment an electrical charge is safely delivered to the face muscles which helps to strengthen them and tighten areas of reduced elasticity, giving a more toned and youthful appearance.
